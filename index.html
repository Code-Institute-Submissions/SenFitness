About

          

SenFitness is about helping you find the true meaning behind strength. About helping you discover your inner strength, embrace it and release it. We want to help you find a passion for training and fitness. We will be here throughout your entire journey to support you. So if you're looking for a challenge and willing to get out your comfort zone, sign up!

SenFitness is a fitness center based in London. We have an exceptional gym with equipment that meet very high standards. We also offer an amazing range of fitness programmes to help you find your perfect training routine.

Monthly Price : £30.

The location is : 27 West Street, North London, N54 8ZV.

       
            

Programmes

Fat Loss

This programme will help you achieve your desired physique and fitness level. This programme will be based more on core compound exercises which will help build muscle and burn fat fast. If you want to become more fit and healthy, this is for you!

MMA

This programme will help you achieve exceptional fighting abilities. This will be based on the basic and complex aspects of Mixed Martial Arts which will help you develop amazing skills and better your quick thinking. So if you're for a challenge, this is for you!

Strength

This programme will help you achieve your desired strength level. This will also utilize every muscle in your body to help them develop! This programme will also require compound exercises and some isolation exercises which can greatly improve your strength!

Calisthenics

This programme is more advanced and will help you achieve great strength and flexibility. Calisthenics focuses on compound exercises and bodyweight training which is an amazing way to increase skill and strength. However, calisthenics is challenging so be ready!

Basic Fitness

This programme will help you get into good health and in a good state of mind. This programme will focus more on simple exercise routines and nutrition. This will definitley help your physical and mental well-being. So, if you want to become healthy this programme is for you!

Maintain

This programme is for those who are happy with their current physique and fitness level. This programme will essentially allow you to decide what type of training you want to do based on your body type and your nutritional habits. So if you want to maintain, this is for you!
